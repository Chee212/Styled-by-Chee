Booking Policies

Please read carefully before booking. By booking an appointment with Styled By Chee, you acknowledge and agree to the policies listed below.

Deposit & Payments

  • A non-refundable $25 deposit is required to secure all appointments.
  • The deposit goes toward your total service cost.
  • The remaining balance must be paid after your appointment.
  • Accepted payment methods will be confirmed at booking.

Hair Prep Requirements

  • Please arrive with your hair washed and thoroughly detangled.
  • Blow-drying is included in the service.
  • Detangling costs an extra $15 if hair is not properly detangled.
  • Hair should be free of heavy oils or product buildup.

Lengths & Style Availability

  • Waist-length and butt-length styles are available.
  • Small styles that require extensions are no longer offered.
  • Please select the correct length and size when booking to avoid delays.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations or reschedules must be made at least 12 hours before your appointment.
  • Late cancellations may result in loss of deposit.
  • No-shows will require a new deposit to rebook.

Late Policy

  • Please arrive on time for your appointment.
  • There is a 20-minute grace period.
  • After 20 minutes, your appointment will be cancelled.
  • Late arrivals may need to reschedule depending on time availability.

Booking & Communication

  • All bookings must be made through the booking site.
  • If needed, a message must be sent before booking for inquiries.
  • Please feel free to ask questions regarding your appointment ahead of time.

Guests & Appointment Etiquette

  • No extra guests are allowed without prior approval.
  • Please do not bring guests to your appointment without my knowledge.
  • This helps ensure a smooth, focused, and timely service.

Refunds

  • Deposits are non-refundable.
  • Refunds are not offered once a service has been completed.
  • If there is an issue, please contact me within 24 hours with clear photos.
