Services

Here are my currently offered Services. Final price may vary by length, size, style etc. Book online to see exact price and service options.

Knotless Braids and Twists

Knotless braids are a modern, lightweight braiding style where extensions are fed in gradually instead of being tied into a knot at the base.

Sizes offered: Smedium / Medium / Large

Stitch Braids

Stitch braids are a sleek cornrow style known for clean, precise parting and a sharp, polished finish.

Can be done with or without extensions.

Twists

Twists are a low-tension protective style created by twisting two strands together.

Can be done with or without extensions.

Single Braids

Single braids are individual, free-hanging braids offering a classic protective style.

Sizes: Small / Smedium / Medium

Butterfly & Soft Locs

Faux loc styles with distressed (butterfly) or smooth (soft) finishes.

Sizes: Smedium / Medium

Fulani Styles

Includes Fulani braids, Fulani sew-ins, and Fulani quick weaves.

With or without extensions.

Ponytail & Updo Styles

Sleek ponytails, buns, and creative updos.

With or without extensions.

Locs

Starter locs using twists, coils or braids

Retwist options offered are: Palmrolling, inter-locking, two-strand retwists and barrel twists

Quick Weaves

A protective style where tracks are bonded onto a wig cap (not directly on your hair) for a sleek sew-in look in less time.

Best for: short-term installs, quick transformations, and low-maintenance styling. Can be done with/without a leave-out.

Sew-ins

A long-lasting install where extensions are sewn onto braided foundation rows for a secure, natural look and versatile styling.

Options: Leave-out sew-in, Closure sew-in.

Pre-Parting

Need clean, straight parts but plan to install the style yourself? I’ll section and pre-part your hair neatly so you can braid or twist at home.

Sizes offered: Small / Smedium / Medium

Line-ups & Fades

Clean, sharp line-ups and tapered fades to refresh your look and keep your cut looking crisp.

Options: Line-up only, Low fade, Mid fade, Taper fade.

Best for a fresh finish after protective styles

Brow Shaping & Tint

Brow shaping to clean up and define your natural brow line, plus optional tint for a fuller, more polished look.
